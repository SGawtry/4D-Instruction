Enter "Lawn & Garden" sample info

Enter sample information for Lawn & Garden samples.

Log into the 4D database and Enter payment and customer info on a new report.
The fields you are entering data into should look like this. Use Tab to navigate between fields.
  1. Press Ctrl + Tab for the number of samples you want to enter.
  2. Click into the first cell of the Lb# field and enter the regular number.
  3. In Sp#, enter the special number if present.
  4. In ,Sm# enter the 4 letter or number sample code if present.
  5. In R1, enter the crop code recommendation, for example "101" for lawns before seeding & sodding.
  6. Type "Y" to check off the following fields as appropriate:
    Field nameTest name and related notes
    Irr (Grass only) Is grass watered regularly?
    Clip (Grass only) Are clippings removed?
    Reg Regular test
    Salt Soluble salts
    Pb Lead
    S Sulfur
    NO3 Nitrate
    B Boron
    CaMg Calcium and magnesium
    Org Defunct organic matter only – do not use
    ZCIM Zinc, copper, iron, and manganese
  7. Repeat steps #2-6 for any additional samples. Ensure there are no extra blank lines at the bottom of the report.
  8. When the payment, customer, and sample information is completely entered, hit the "Accept" button in the upper right-hand corner of the window.
