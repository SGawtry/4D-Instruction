Enter payment and customer info on a new report

Enter the information on the top half of the 4D data entry form. To enter information for the bottom half of the form, see either Enter "Farm & Field" sample info or Enter "Lawn & Garden" sample info.

You must be logged into the database. See Log into the 4D database.
Hit Tab to navigate between (most) of the menu options on the top half of the screen. Type in all capital letters for easier reading by postal scanners.
  1. In the top drop-down menu, select Task and then either Order Entry – Farm Field or Order Entry – Lawn & Garden depending on the report type.
    A window will pop up with the following top half:

  2. Click into the Pmt field and enter the dollar amount. If the client will be billed later, leave it at $0.00 and skip to step 5.
  3. In the Typ field, select either Cash or Check for payment type and click OK. .

    Note: Credit cards are entered under check

  4. For the Ck# field, enter the check number or "CC" followed by the last 4 digits of the credit card number found on their receipt. Cash payments are left clank If an order is overpaid, underpaid, or there is another special situation, write a quick note as seen in the examples below

    Note: If they over- or under- paid by $5 or more, do not enter the form and inform Stephanie.

    SituationNote
    Client underpaid #2 on check #8475 8476 U2
    Client paid with cash and used a $2 off coupon COUPON2
    Client ordered soil package #1 on the Pro Turf Management Form and received a $1.50 discount off their check #2222 payment 2222 PTM150
    Client paid $15 with check #3333 and $2 in cash. 3333 CASH2
  5. Hit Tab until you get to the CType field and select the most appropriate option for the customer.
  6. Hit Tab until you get to the Ph (phone number) box and enter the client's phone number. Hit Tab. If nothing happens, continue to step 7. If a pop-up window appears, complete the following steps:
    1. From the pop-up window, select the correct address from the list and hit OK. If the correct address does not exist, hit "Cancel" and skip to step 7.
    2. Go back to the Firm field and enter the company name if present. If there is no company, enter the client's name, first then last, and hit Tab.
    3. Select the correct account from the list. If you are unsure, choose the most current account (e.g. largest account number).
    4. Confirm that their name and address is correct. If yes, skip to step 9. If no, go back to 6b and choose a new account.
  7. Enter the client's name and mailing address on the left side of the screen. Leave the shorter Add1 box blank in all situations. If there is no second address line, leave Add2 blank. Do not type any text into ZIP4.

    Note: The mailing name and address appear on different sides of the "Lawn & Garden" and "Farm & Field" forms. Make sure you enter the correct mailing information and not the optional reference information.

  8. For AType (account type), select either "Payment" for upfront payment (most common) or "Credit" for clients that will be billed later.
  9. If there is an optional reference name or address, enter that information in a similar manner on the right half of the window. It is acceptable to have only a name or an incomplete address.

    Note: Make sure that the optional reference's name does not exactly match the mailing address name, or the optional address will not print. Abbreviate the optional first name to a first initial if needed.

  10. Click to the County field, type the county name, and hit Tab. The Code field to the right will automatically fill.
  11. In Town, enter one of the following:
    • For Lawn & Garden forms, skip the next Code box and type in "88".
    • For Farm & Field forms, enter the township name if provided. Otherwise, type "99U" and hit Tab.
  12. Tab to the Report No. box and enter the report number.
  13. In the Charge field, use "Prepaid" for prepaid clients, or "To Account" for credit (e.g. bill later) clients.
  14. Go to the Enter "Farm & Field" sample info or Enter "Lawn & Garden" sample info link to continue entering this order. Do NOT alter the Date Rec'd under any circumstances.
